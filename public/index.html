Frequently Asked Questions

Is the gold electroplating real?
Yes. We use pure 24-karat gold electroplating over brass. Each product comes with a certificate of gold purity and electroplating thickness.
How do I clean and maintain the idol?
Do not use chemical cleaners, scrubs, or water. Since the idols are protected by a thick lacquer coating, you only need to wipe them gently with a dry, soft micro-fiber cloth once in a while.
Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?
Yes, we support Cash on Delivery across most pin codes in India. We also offer secure online payments including UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking with extra discounts.
What if the idol is damaged in transit?
We secure all shipments with robust multi-layered packing and wooden crates. We offer a 100% free replacement guarantee if your idol gets damaged in transit. Simply record an unboxing video when opening the parcel.
